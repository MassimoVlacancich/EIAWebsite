Our Platform

Our staff is currently working on the new Luminary platform to open everything for discussion.

Development

Nearly one year ago, a small team of talented engineers was recluted to begin the development of Luminary. Through the use of modern web design techiniques, we were able to design an innovative and easy to use social network

Features

It will be possible to take advantage of the Luminary platform in several ways. From reading articles to starting campaigns, from browsing a political dictionary to taking quizzes, raising awareness about the causes that you care about the most has never been so easy.

Beta Version

The Beta version of the platform will be available for new users on request starting Mid-June 2017. The public release will be annunced later during the year.

Content

In the meanwhile, you can start reading the original content curated by our staff in the articles (Beta) section and follow us on our social pages!

OUR VISION

Our world is changing. Everyone adapts everyday to the systematic shifts that our society goes through. But people like you and us can be the driver for the change towards a more open a fair society.

Events

The political events of 2016 have been pivotal in defining a new era where democracy is constantly challenged.

Voices

But it is also an era where our voices can make a difference. We want this possibility to become a reality.

Community

We want to funnel our community potential into a protected and unified platform from which we can spur the change the world needs. Together.

We envision Luminary to be such place. It will be the platform where our voices will not just make a differente. They will be the difference.
